Our Atelier

Rooted in Tradition,
Refined for Today

Founded in Lagos, ÀṢỌ is a house built on the belief that African heritage dressing is not a throwback — it is the future. We work exclusively with Yoruba master weavers, Igbo embroiderers, and Northern Hausa tailors to produce pieces that carry genuine cultural authority.

Each commission begins with a conversation. We learn how a man moves, where he leads, what he carries. Then we dress him accordingly.

What Sets Us Apart

The Craft

Hand-woven Fabrics

Every aso-oke and sanyan cloth is sourced directly from loom masters in Iseyin and Ilorin — never commercial.

6–8 Week Bespoke

No shortcuts. Every commission takes the time it deserves. We refuse to compromise on the hours a great piece requires.

Home Fitting Service

Our tailors come to you in Lagos, Abuja, London, and Houston. Bespoke should feel effortless from start to finish.

Heritage Preservation

10% of every sale funds apprenticeships for young weavers across the southwest, ensuring the tradition survives another generation.
